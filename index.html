    • Project 2025

    *Presidential Transition Project*, is a collection of policy proposals to fundamentally restructure the United States federal government in the event of a Republican victory in the 2024 presidential election. The goal of the project is to create a conservative policy agenda, personnel database, training program, and action plan for the first 180 days of the next administration.
